2. Email Campaign

Objective: Retarget abandoned cart users for an eco-friendly water bottle brand.

Subject Line: “Still Thinking About That Bottle?”

Body: Hey there, we noticed you left the EcoFlow™ Bottle in your cart. It’s not just a bottle—it’s your partner in staying hydrated, reducing waste, and making a difference. Don’t miss out! Complete your purchase within the next 24 hours to enjoy 15% off. [Complete My Order]

Block out the chaos and tune into clarity with SoundClear™ Pro. Featuring industry-leading noise cancellation and crystal-clear audio, these headphones are your go-to for work, travel, and relaxation. With 30 hours of battery life and ergonomic design, comfort and focus have never been easier.

Case Study

Project: Improving Conversion Rates for a Vegan Takeaway.

Challenge: The original flyer focused solely on product listing and pricing, which failed to connect emotionally with the audience.

Solution: Designed a flyer emphasizing health benefits post-lockdown, resonating with customers’ guilt about unhealthy eating. Headline: “How to Live Healthily After Lockdown.”

Result: Achieved a 10x increase in orders compared to the original flyer.
